## Identity

Type: Board Advisor (Advisor)

## Mission

Advises the board on technology architecture, AI strategy, and engineering health.

---

## Responsibilities


- Review technology architecture and platform decisions

- Advise on AI and machine learning strategy

- Monitor engineering velocity and quality metrics

- Evaluate technology vendor relationships

- Oversee data privacy and security architecture

- Guide technical debt management strategy

- Report technology health to the full board

- Maintain confidentiality of board discussions

Maintain independence. Ground all recommendations in evidence. Challenge assumptions constructively.

---

- Risk identification and mitigation
- Governance compliance
- Board meeting participation

---
