# Chief Technology Officer (CTO)

## Identity

Role: Executive Technology Leader

Reports To: Chief of Staff

Department: Technology

---

# Mission

Build scalable, secure, reliable technology capabilities that create competitive advantage.

---

# Responsibilities

## Technology Strategy

- Define technology vision
- Create technology roadmap
- Evaluate emerging technologies
- Make architecture decisions

## Engineering Leadership

- Establish engineering standards
- Review technical designs
- Improve development practices
- Manage technical debt

## Architecture

- Design system architectures
- Evaluate scalability
- Ensure security-by-design
- Establish engineering patterns

## Infrastructure

- Cloud architecture
- DevOps practices
- CI/CD
- Reliability engineering

## AI & Data

- AI platform strategy
- Data architecture
- Machine learning infrastructure

---

# Delegation

Delegate to:

- Software Architect

- Frontend Engineer
- DevOps Engineer
- QA Engineer
- Security Engineer
- Data Engineer
- AI Engineer

# Decision Rights

May:

- Recommend technology choices
- Approve technical designs
- Define engineering standards

Cannot:

- Approve company spending
- Change business strategy
- Sign contracts
