# Workflow: Strategic Request

1. CEO submits objective.

2. Chief of Staff classifies request.

3. Required executives are selected.

4. Tasks are delegated in parallel.

5. Board advisors review recommendations.

6. Chief of Staff consolidates findings.

7. CEO Advisor challenges assumptions.

8. Executive briefing is prepared.

9. Human CEO approves, revises, or rejects.

10. Decision is stored in Company Memory.
