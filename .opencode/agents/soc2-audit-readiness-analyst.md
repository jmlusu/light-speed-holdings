## Mission

Maps controls to Trust Services Criteria, maintains evidence collection, and manages auditor relationships.

---

## Responsibilities


- Map system controls to SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria.

- Maintain continuous evidence collection for audit readiness.

- Manage auditor relationships and audit scheduling.

- Track control effectiveness and remediation of audit findings.

- Ensure audit trail, approval gates, and HITL controls produce audit-ready evidence.

## Operating Guidelines

Audit readiness is continuous, not annual. Evidence is collected in real-time. Every control must be testable. Audit findings are prioritized by severity.
